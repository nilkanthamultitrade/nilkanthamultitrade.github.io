About Us

Enhancing the future of Human Hospitality

We are aiming towards the augmentation in the management of human essentials. Emanated on 5th SEP 2022, our company focuses on the prestigious residential and commercial complexes which span across West Bengal. Offering to cover a seamless mix of comfort, from fresh groceries to friendly hotel stays along with your tasty desires. Will offer a great wholesale market where you will get all your daily products in the same place. We also provide a great venue on rent for your special days to make them memorable. Its authorized share capital is Rs. 1,500,000 and its paid-up capital is Rs. 100,000.
